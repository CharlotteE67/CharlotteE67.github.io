Research Interest

My research interests include Urban Computing and Data Mining, which mainly utilize Deep Learning techniques to solve real-world challenges, especially for traffic environment. Recently, my research has specifically focused on the following directions:

  • Spatial-temporal data generation (e.g., traffic trajectory generation).
  • Machine unlearning (Post-process for data generation method to strengthen the privacy-preserving system.).
  • Representation learning (e.g., driver behaivor representation learning).
